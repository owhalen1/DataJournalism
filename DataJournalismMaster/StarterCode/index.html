Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Click to explore associations between demographics and behavioral risk factors. Data collected is from the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) during the year 2014. Click on any of the axis titles!

An inverse association between the household income and smoking suggests that dependencies are due to low income. Similar association was observed between age dependency and alcohol consumption. We also can see an inverse association between poverty and being physically active.
